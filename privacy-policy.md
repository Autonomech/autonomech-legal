# Privacy Policy for Autonomech

**Last updated: December 17, 2024**

### 1. Introduction
Welcome to Autonomech ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our AI-powered vehicle repair assistant application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

### 2. Information We Collect

**2.1 Account Information**
When you create an account, we collect:
• Email address
• Password (encrypted)
• Account creation date

**2.2 Vehicle Information**
To provide personalized repair guidance, we collect:
• Vehicle Identification Number (VIN)
• Vehicle make, model, and year
• Engine specifications
• OBD diagnostic codes (when connected)

**2.3 Camera and Microphone Data**
Our App uses your device's camera and microphone to provide AR-guided repair assistance:
• Camera: Used for real-time object detection and AR overlays during repairs. Images are processed locally and/or sent to our AI services for analysis. We do not permanently store camera images.
• Microphone: Used for voice commands and communication with the AI assistant. Audio is processed in real-time and may be transcribed for AI interaction purposes.

**2.4 Repair Session Data**
We collect information about your repair sessions including:
• Repair tasks performed
• Progress and completion status
• Voice interaction transcripts
• Session timestamps

### 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:
• Provide and maintain our AI repair assistance services
• Personalize repair guidance for your specific vehicle
• Improve our AI models and service quality
• Process and manage your subscription
• Communicate with you about service updates
• Ensure the security of our services

### 4. Data Storage and Security
Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase for data storage, which provides enterprise-grade security including:
• Encryption at rest and in transit
• Regular security audits
• Secure authentication protocols

### 5. Third-Party Services
Our App integrates with third-party services including:
• Google AI (Gemini) for AI-powered assistance
• Supabase for data storage and authentication
• Superwall for subscription management
• Apple Sign In and Google Sign In for authentication

These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

### 6. Your Rights
You have the right to:
• Access your personal data
• Request deletion of your account and data
• Update or correct your information
• Opt out of non-essential data collection
• Export your data

### 7. Data Retention
We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

### 8. Children's Privacy
Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

### 9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
